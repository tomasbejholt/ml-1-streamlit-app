# L08: Project Assistance

No new content. Dedicated to working through assignments from L1-L7, answering questions, and reviewing progress before diving into image recognition.

## Why a Break Here

Tabular ML (MLPs + trees) needs to solidify before switching to a fundamentally different data domain. Rushing into images without a solid tabular foundation creates confusion. The jump from "rows of numbers" to "grids of pixels" is conceptually large — give it breathing room.

## What to Cover

- Review projects, help debug
- Answer accumulated questions from L1-L7
- Revisit concepts that didn't land the first time
- Preview: "Next lesson we switch to images — same training loop, completely different data"
